Magyk

Author: Angie Sage

Genre: Fantasy

Published: March 2005

Description

Magyk is the first book in the Septimus Heap series, following the adventures of Jenna, a young princess raised as a common girl, and Septimus, a boy raised in the Young Army who discovers he is the seventh son of a seventh son. When their worlds collide, they embark on a thrilling journey filled with magic, mystery, and danger. With the help of Marcia Overstrand, the ExtraOrdinary Wizard, they must escape the clutches of evil forces and discover their true destinies in a richly detailed magical world.

Review
